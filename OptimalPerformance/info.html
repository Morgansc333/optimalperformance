Information and how to use the website

Introduction

On this web page there is multiple things you can do as a user

Home page

On the home page you can use the calculators to workout your BMI (Body mass Index) or your maintenance calories in order to manage your daily intake. To do this enter your height and weight into the labelled boxes then proceed to click the submit button, below the submit button will be the results.

Diary page

Once you have selected diary page you will have a number of boxes you can enter data into, first select the date of your workout in the calendar box, after this proceed to enter the exercise you carried out and how many sets, reps and weight of the exercise once you have completed this think about how hard that exercise was on a scale of 1-10. Enter this rating into the RPE box this piece of data will be very important later, after this has been entered click the Add button. Your exercise has now been added and will be displayed below add as many exercise as you like and you can use the date drop menu below to filter your workouts by the date you carried them out on

Logging out

Logging out of your account is very simple on the top navigation bar on the far right is a button that says log out all you have to do it click it and it will log out you out and take you back the log in page.
